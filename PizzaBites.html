Recipe: Gluten Free Pizza Bites

Its Pizza Time

Posted by Kaelin Dickerson on August 24, 2019

Gluten free pizza is a delicious thing. Gluten free pizza bites are just as delicious but in a small package, so how can you go wrong right? Well this was my logic when I decided to look for a recipe on Pinterest.

One day I had to find something to make to last me the entire week, and I had no clue what to make. I wanted to find something easy and relatively cheap. For some reason I decided to open Pinterest (I’m not a recipe maker just yet) and look for gluten free pizza bites. Boy I am glad I did. The recipe is very easy and straightforward, so it shouldn’t require too much cooking prowess.

Ingredients:

  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
  2. Prepare biscuits according to Bisquick instructions.
  3. Cut biscuits in half.
  4. Place first half of biscuit in muffin tray.
  5. Layer with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  6. Place second half of dough on top of pizza filling.
  7. Top again with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes.
  9. Cool for 5 minutes.

See nice and simple. Now I deviated from the recipe a little bit by adding some parmesan cheese, and peppers to mine, but to each their own. Make these for a quick little snack, or for the main course. Honestly they are the perfect size for either.
